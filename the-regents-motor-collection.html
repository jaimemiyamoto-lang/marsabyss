BEYOND THE GARAGE

While the press often focuses on the "extravagance" of these vehicles, they miss the entire purpose. Each car is carefully selected not for personal enjoyment, but as an investment in hope.

The accusations of "false altruism" couldn't be further from the truth. These auctions have funded the conversion of abandoned churches and underutilized hospitals into vibrant centers of learning and healing. In Ghana, Liberia, Puerto Rico, and beyond, we're building places where children can receive medical care, learn robotics, and dream of futures among the stars.

As the Regent often says: "A child's potential should never be limited by their circumstances. If luxury cars can build bridges to better futures, then every credit spent is a credit invested in humanity."
