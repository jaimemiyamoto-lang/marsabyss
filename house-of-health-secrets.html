THE HOUSE OF HEALTH SECRETS

"Abandon Hope All Ye Who Enter Here... For When You Do, You Will Learn The Truth"

"And ye shall know the truth, and the truth shall make you free." - John 8:32 (KJV). Within these digital walls lie the grim realities our ancestors fought to conquer. What you see here made me take care of my health. When they saw me working out outside my house, they said: 'He's on to something.' Now you will understand why.

Anthrax

20-80% Mortality Rate
Spores Decades-Lasting
Symptoms:
  • Skin ulcers with black centers
  • Fever and chest discomfort
  • Severe breathing problems
  • Shock and meningitis

Diphtheria

5-10% Mortality Rate
Thick Coating Blocks Airway
Symptoms:
  • Thick gray throat coating
  • Sore throat and swollen glands
  • Weakness and nerve damage
  • Heart muscle inflammation

Hepatitis A

1.5M Annual Cases
Fecal-Oral Transmission
Symptoms:
  • Fatigue and nausea
  • Abdominal pain and loss of appetite
  • Jaundice (yellow skin/eyes)
  • Dark urine and clay-colored stools

Hepatitis B

887K Annual Deaths
Chronic Liver Disease
Symptoms:
  • Jaundice and abdominal pain
  • Joint pain and fatigue
  • Liver cirrhosis and failure
  • Liver cancer risk

Hib (Haemophilus influenzae type b)

3-6% Mortality Rate
Children Under 5 Years
Symptoms:
  • Meningitis (brain inflammation)
  • Pneumonia and epiglottitis
  • Septic arthritis
  • Cellulitis and sepsis

HPV (Human Papillomavirus)

80% Sexually Active
630M Global Infections
Symptoms:
  • Genital warts
  • Cervical, throat, anal cancers
  • Often asymptomatic initially
  • Precancerous lesions

Influenza

290K-650K Annual Deaths
3-5M Severe Cases
Symptoms:
  • High fever and cough
  • Muscle aches and fatigue
  • Pneumonia complications
  • Respiratory failure

Measles

1 in 1000 Brain Inflammation
2.6M Annual Deaths (1960s)
Symptoms:
  • High fever and cough
  • Widespread rash
  • Pneumonia and encephalitis
  • Permanent brain damage

Meningococcal Disease

10-15% Mortality Rate
Hours Rapid Progression
Symptoms:
  • Sudden high fever and headache
  • Stiff neck and sensitivity to light
  • Rash that doesn't fade under pressure
  • Septic shock and organ failure

Mpox (Monkeypox)

1-10% Mortality Rate
Painful Skin Lesions
Symptoms:
  • Fever and headache
  • Swollen lymph nodes
  • Rash progressing to painful blisters
  • Scarring after healing

Mumps

1 in 4 Males: Orchitis
1 in 20 Pancreatitis
Symptoms:
  • Painful swollen salivary glands
  • Fever and headache
  • Muscle aches and fatigue
  • Meningitis and hearing loss

Pertussis (Whooping Cough)

160K Annual Deaths
10 Weeks Cough Duration
Symptoms:
  • Severe coughing fits
  • "Whooping" sound when breathing
  • Vomiting after coughing
  • Pneumonia and seizures

Pneumococcal Disease

1.6M Annual Deaths
Children Most Vulnerable
Symptoms:
  • Pneumonia (lung infection)
  • Meningitis (brain infection)
  • Sepsis (blood infection)
  • Ear infections and sinusitis

Polio

5-10% Paralysis Rate
500K Yearly Cases (Pre-Vaccine)
Symptoms:
  • Muscle weakness and pain
  • Permanent paralysis
  • Respiratory failure
  • Limb deformities

Rabies

~100% Fatal Once Symptomatic
59K Annual Deaths
Symptoms:
  • Anxiety and confusion
  • Hallucinations and hydrophobia
  • Excessive salivation
  • Paralysis and coma

Rotavirus

215K Annual Deaths
Children Under 5 Years
Symptoms:
  • Severe watery diarrhea
  • Vomiting and fever
  • Abdominal pain
  • Dehydration requiring hospitalization

Rubella (German Measles)

CRS Birth Defects
Pregnant High Risk
Symptoms:
  • Rash starting on face
  • Low-grade fever
  • Swollen lymph nodes
  • Congenital rubella syndrome in newborns

Smallpox

30% Mortality Rate
300M+ 20th Century Deaths
Symptoms:
  • High fever and vomiting
  • Rash turning to pus-filled sores
  • Deep scarring and blindness
  • Organ failure

Tetanus

11% Mortality Rate
100% Pain Level
Symptoms:
  • Muscle stiffness and spasms
  • Lockjaw (trismus)
  • Fractures from muscle contractions
  • Respiratory paralysis

Varicella (Chickenpox)

4.2M Annual Cases (Pre-Vaccine)
100-150 Annual Deaths (US)
Symptoms:
  • Itchy blister-like rash
  • Fever and fatigue
  • Skin infections and scarring
  • Pneumonia and encephalitis

Zoster (Shingles)

1 in 3 Lifetime Risk
Postherpetic Neuralgia
Symptoms:
  • Painful blistering rash
  • Burning, tingling sensation
  • Fever and headache
  • Long-term nerve pain
