The Night That Changed Everything üåô

This page is dedicated to the memory of a Massachusetts state trooper who lost his life in the line of duty. Though I never knew him personally, our paths crossed on a night that could have ended very differently.

.

While I survived that night,and as his spirit passed through me, he became the catalyst for my return to writing.

I've mentioned fragments of this experience on social media, but this is the first time I'm sharing the full context. His sacrifice reminded me of the fragility of life and the importance of using whatever time we have to create, to express, and to honor those who give everything.

This page is my small tribute not just to him, but to all service members and first responders who put themselves in harm's way for the safety of others. Their courage echoes across time and space, even reaching as far as Mars.
