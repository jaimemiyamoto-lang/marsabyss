THE FIRST 13 MARTIAN COLONIES

Ares Prime

The first and largest colony, named after the Greek god of war and the planet itself.

New Boston

Cultural and educational hub, continuing Massachusetts' legacy of learning.

Salem Dome

Philosophical center, honoring Salem's history of intellectual freedom.

Madrid Dome

Spanish cultural center with authentic architecture and Mediterranean climate.

Shanghai Dome

Asian technology hub specializing in robotics and sustainable energy.

Riyadh Dome

Desert adaptation research center pioneering water reclamation tech.

Olympus Metro

Built into the foothills of Olympus Mons, specializing in geological research.

Valles City

Agricultural heartland producing 60% of Mars' food supply in Mariner Valley.

Dubai-do City

Luxury district featuring the tallest structures and artificial islands on Mars.

New York Dome

Financial center housing the Martian Stock Exchange and banking institutions.

Beverly Dome

Entertainment district with holographic theaters and zero-gravity concert halls.

Tokyo Dome

Robotics and AI development center where 60% of workforce are advanced AI systems.

Paris Dome

Arts district preserving Earth's artistic traditions while pioneering Martian expression.
