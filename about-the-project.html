ABOUT THE PROJECT

Discover the vision behind The Mars Abyss - an ambitious sci-fi universe blending anthology storytelling with serialized adventures

THE VISION

The Mars Abyss is more than a story‚Äîit's an immersive science fiction experience that transports you to a future where humanity has colonized Mars, but at a profound cost. Our mission is to create compelling narratives that explore the human condition through the lens of science fiction and fantasy.

Imagine a universe where stories unfold like The Twilight Zone meets Black Mirror, where each episode can stand alone as a powerful commentary on technology, society, and human nature, while also contributing to a larger, interconnected narrative.

Anthology Series

Weekly self-contained episodes with different characters and scenarios, each exploring unique philosophical and technological concepts

Serialized Adventure

Continuous story arcs following beloved characters through their journeys across Mars and alternative realities

Interactive Experience

Games, virtual experiences, and community engagement that bring the universe to life beyond traditional storytelling

THE JOURNEY: 64 YEARS IN THE MAKING

1970s ‚Äî 1980s: Writing love poems, and TV comedy sketches in a house full of imagination.

This isn't a new dream - it's a lifetime in the making. Back in the 1970s, living in a humble house in the Puerto Rican countryside with curtains for doors and mosquito netting around my bed, I was already writing the stories that would eventually become The Mars Abyss.

We had a barn door for a front door and a latrine out back, but what we lacked in material wealth, we made up for in imagination. Every mosquito buzzing outside that netting was a spaceship, every star visible through the cracks in the wooden walls was a distant planet waiting to be explored.

I still miss those days, and that house!

üìú The Letter That Kept Me Going, which more or less, went like this:

"Although we cannot publish your three stories at this time, we were impressed by your imagination and strongly encourage you to continue writing. The world needs more storytellers with your vision. And we certainly got a kick out of the last one."

- The Twilight Zone Magazine, 1980s

That rejection letter became my fuel. Now at 64, I'm still pursuing those same dreams, now more mature and with better stories - it seems now could at last be the right time, with a lifetime of experience and the technology to bring them to life.

"Your past doesn't define your limitations - it defines your resilience."

THE COUNTERPART UNIVERSE

At the heart of our narrative lies a fascinating premise: M.A.I. (Master Artificial Intelligence) has devised a counterpart universe where Puerto Rico, also known as 'El Nuevo Imperio del Reino Borincano', Spain, and The Oriental Hemisphere shepherd humanity toward an almost utopian future.

By sending the robot XJ-9 back to Puerto Rico's colonial times, M.A.I. created a parallel dimension where certain events unfolded differently, resulting in a world distinct from our own‚Äîa world where cooperation and innovation led to unprecedented human advancement.

Alternative History

Explore how small changes in the past create dramatically different futures across dimensions

Cultural Fusion

Puerto Rican, Spanish, and Oriental influences creating a unique societal blend in a parallel reality

Technological Utopia

Advanced AI guiding humanity toward prosperity while preserving cultural diversity across dimensions
