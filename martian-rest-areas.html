MARTIAN REST AREAS

Oasis of Comfort on the Long Martian Journey

THE VERMONT OF MARS INITIATIVE

Inspired by Earth's highway rest areas - particularly those in the Northeast & Northwest areas, where travelers find respite on long journeys - the Martian Colonial Authority has established a network of fully-equipped rest areas across major transit routes.

These facilities provide weary inter-dome travelers with showers, hot meals, comfortable sleeping quarters, and recreational facilities - all free of charge to promote safe travel across the Martian landscape.
