FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What happens if I get sick during the journey? +
Every spacecraft has a fully equipped medical bay with AI doctors. Common space sickness is treated with medication, and serious conditions can be stabilized until reaching Mars medical facilities.
Can I communicate with Earth during the journey? +
Yes! Quantum entanglement communication allows real-time messaging. Video calls experience 3-5 minute delays due to distance, but text is instantaneous.
What about food allergies or dietary restrictions? +
The MAI system tracks all passenger dietary needs. 3D food printers can create meals matching any dietary requirement - just ensure this information is in your pre-travel profile.
Is there internet access on Mars? +
Mars has its own high-speed network called MarsNet. Earth internet is accessible but may have delays. Your accommodation includes complimentary high-speed access.
What if I change my mind and want to return early? +
Early return is possible but subject to seat availability on returning spacecraft. Standard change fees apply. Travel insurance is recommended.
