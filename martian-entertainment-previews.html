The Story of Silver Saint Claire

Created in the early 2000s, Silver Saint Claire is a sentient robot with a soul - a being of remarkable depth and personality. Her sleek silver form conceals a complex consciousness that values efficiency, morality, and surprisingly... frugality.

Though equipped with a powerful rocket pack that allows her to fly, Silver prefers her jetbike for everyday travel. "Why waste precious rocket fuel when you can enjoy the wind in your face?" she often quips. This practical, almost stingy nature hides a fierce protective instinct - she'll unleash her full rocket power without hesitation if her "boyfriend's" life is in immediate danger.

In our Martian saga, Silver serves as a voice of reason against corporate excess. When "The Over Stimulator" - an Overseer of Earth obsessed with efficiency at all costs - arrives to replace 3,000 human employees with maintenance-free robots, Silver becomes the unexpected champion of humanity. Her opposition isn't just about saving jobs; it's about preserving the soul of the workplace.
